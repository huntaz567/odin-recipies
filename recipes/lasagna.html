Homemade Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, cheese, and meat sauce. It's a hearty meal perfect for family gatherings or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Meat: 1/2 pound ground beef, 1/2 pound ground pork
  • Onion: 1 medium, diced, cooked with meat
  • Canned Tomatoes: 1 can tomato sauce, 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • Fresh Herbs: 2 tablespoons parsley, 1 crushed clove of garlic
  • Sugar: a dash of sugar balances out the acidity
  • Spices and seasonings: Dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper
  • Noodles: Lasagna Noodles!
  • Cheese: Cottage, Parmesan, and Shredded Mozzarella
  • Eggs: to make the cheese layer extra creamy

How to Make the Homemade lasagna

  1. Cook the meat: Cook the ground meat in a skillet until browned and crumbly. Add the onion and continue cooking until it's translucent. Stir in the canned tomato products, half of the parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoons of salt, oregano, and sugar.
  2. Cook the Noodles: Boil the lasagna noodles in lightly salted water until they are al dente
  3. Make the cheese layer: Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, the remaining parsley, the remaining salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Assemble the lasagna: Layer the ingredients according to the recipe (starting with sauce and ending with mozzarella) until the lasagna is assembled.
  5. Bake the lasagna: Cover the lasagna with foil and bake in a preheated oven for 30 minutes Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
