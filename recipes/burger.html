Burger Recipe

The perfect basic burger

This classic burger recipe is simple and delicious, perfect for any cookout or weeknight dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Large Egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 Pound Ground Beef
  • 1/2 cup fine dry breadcrumbs

Instructions

  1. Preheat outdoor grill for high heat. Lightly oil grate
  2. Whisk egg, salt, and pepper together in a medium bowl
  3. Add ground beef and breadcrumbs to the egg mixture; mix until well combined
  4. Form mixture into 4 3/4-inch-thick patties
  5. Place patties on the preheated grill. Cover and cook 6 to 8 minutes per side, or to desired doneness. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
  6. Serve and enjoy!
